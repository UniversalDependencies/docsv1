det: determiner

The relation determiner (det) holds between a nominal head and its determiner:

Am văzut un okapi . \n Have-I seen an okapi .
det(okapi, un)

Spune-i lui Ion . \n Tell-him the-Dative  John.
det(Ion, lui)
